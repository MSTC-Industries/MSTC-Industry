About MSTC

We are a group of skilled programmers at the Center for Information Technology in Deep Run High School. We help small businesses by creating a useful and high quality websites for customer outreach and interaction.

Why us?

Our websites are hand coded and thus are of much higher quality than any website building service. For you, there is no frusteration with an unfriendly Wix or SquareSpace UI or something you don't know how to do; we take care of it. For your customers there are no strange bugs or backend errors.

Price

Platforms like wix charge up to 159 dollars a month for basic features, and that still only includes one year of a free domain. We won't charge you an arm and a leg instead tailoring your price to what you need.

Upkeep

We won't just make your website, help you choose a domain and hosting plan but we will always be one call away in case you want a simple change or a large overhaul.
